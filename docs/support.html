Privacy policy

Frequently asked questions

How do I export my entries?
Open Settings (gear icon) → Export. You can export as PDF or CSV.
Can I lock the app?
Yes. In Settings → Privacy, turn on App Lock and use Face ID or Touch ID.
Where is my data stored?
Only on your iPhone or iPad. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.
